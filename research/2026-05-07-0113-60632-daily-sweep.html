Job Hunter’s Best Strategies — 60632

⭐ Get forklift certified — $18-25/hr — Certification costs $100-200, takes 1 day. Temp agencies prioritize certified operators. Golden ticket to warehouse work
⭐ Walk 45th & Tripp industrial core — hire at gate — Bagcraft, Vitner's and others often post 'Help Wanted' signs. Show up and ask for hiring manager. Many don't post online
⭐ Midway Airport — apply to vendors, not airport — Southwest, Delta, HMSHost, SSP America, rental car companies, TSA, cargo handlers. Apply directly to those companies.
⭐ Temp-to-hire = primary conversion path — Most mfg/warehouse hiring starts temp. Show up on time for 90 days. Temp agency IS your interview.
⭐ PeopleReady Archer Ave — same-day work — 3924 S Archer Ave. Walk in, get industrial work same day. Use JobStack app. Temp-to-hire conversion after 90 days.
⭐ CDL + warehouse = career path — GEODIS, Vitner's, Bagcraft need drivers + warehouse. Class B/A CDL + warehouse experience = highly employable.
⭐ Industrial construction pipeline = jobs — Sterling Bay, Brookfield/Western Works, Bridge Industrial = construction jobs NOW, permanent jobs LATER.
⭐ Search 'Midway' and '60632' on job boards — Set alerts on LinkedIn/Indeed/Glassdoor. 1,800+ warehouse openings near 60632. Apply same-day.
▫ 14th Ward office — call for leads — Alderwoman Gutierrez (773-236-0117). Ask which businesses have new permits — those are likely hiring.
▫ GSDC — NOF grant recipients hire — Neighborhood Opportunity Fund grants up to $250K. Call GSDC 773-436-1000 for list of recipients.
