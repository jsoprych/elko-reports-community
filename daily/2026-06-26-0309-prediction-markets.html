📚 What Are Prediction Markets? click to expand

Prediction markets let people bet real money on future events — elections, Fed rate decisions, recession odds, even F1 championships. The price of each bet reflects the crowd's collective probability: a "Yes" share trading at 52¢ means the market believes there's a 52% chance the event happens.

They work because money talks louder than polls. When traders put real capital behind their beliefs, the resulting odds tend to be more accurate than expert forecasts alone. Studies consistently show prediction markets outperform traditional polling and punditry.
