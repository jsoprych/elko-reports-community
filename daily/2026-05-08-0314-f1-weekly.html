Gilles Villeneuve's Tragic Passing (8 May 1982)
On this day in 1982, legendary Ferrari driver Gilles Villeneuve died in a crash during qualifying for the Belgian Grand Prix at Zolder. The Canadian won 6 Grands Prix in his career and is remembered as one of F1's most charismatic and fearless drivers.
