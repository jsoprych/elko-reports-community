Licensing Requirements

  • City of Chicago Journeyman Plumber License — Required for work within Chicago city limits. Issued by Dept. of Buildings. Alternative: State of Illinois Plumber's License is also recognized if employer holds Chicago contractor license.
  • State of Illinois Plumber License — Issued by IDPH. Valid statewide, including Chicago when working for a City-licensed contractor.
  • Apprentice Plumber License — Required for apprentices. Both City and State issue these.
  • Plumber license renewal fees (2026): $100/year if expired. Reinstatement requires additional fees.
