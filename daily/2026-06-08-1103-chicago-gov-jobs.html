Application Portal Guides

Step-by-step guides for applying to each employer. Timelines vary widely: USPS can hire in 2-4 weeks, while rail and government roles can take 1-6 months.
USPS
  • Create profile at jobs.usps.com
  • Complete the 473 exam for mail processing/carrier roles
  • Background check + drug screen required
  • USPS hires fast — can go from application to offer in 2-4 weeks
  • Heavy hiring periods: Oct-Dec (holiday), May-Aug (summer)
Amtrak
  • Create profile at careers.amtrak.com
  • Chicago is a major Amtrak hub — check regularly
  • Applications can sit for 1-6 months — apply and forget
  • Focus on: station services, custodial, warehouse, platform attendant
Metra
  • Apply at metra.com/employment
  • Station attendant and custodial roles open periodically
  • Drug screen + physical exam typical
City / County / State
  • City of Chicago: chicago.gov/careers (residency required)
  • Cook County: cookcountyil.taleo.net (3 sections: 100/200/400)
  • State of Illinois: illinois.jobs2web.com (SAP portal)
  • Government processes are slow — 2-6 months typical
Federal (USAJOBS)
  • USAJOBS.gov requires separate profile with resume builder
  • Use 'entry level' filter + Chicago location
  • Federal hiring can take 3-9 months
  • Veterans preference applies at all federal agencies
